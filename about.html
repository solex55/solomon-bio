I am a freelancer in frontend development, cyber security analyst, affiliate marketer, content developer and crypto enthusiast.

About Me

In my short time of working in the IT environment I have ventured into the software developing world, where I learnt how to implement software solutions by building web applications. I wrote and test code, often using development tools. This role involved talking to clients and colleagues to assess and define what solution of system is needed, this helped me increase my verbal and communication skills as well as technical work. I have also served in the role of an IT support analysts who provides technical set-up, support and advice to IT users via email, phone, social media and in person. I either provided support within a particular organisation or to external businesses, customers of a particular product or on an ad hoc basis. While obeying the clarion call as a corper, I served in the role of a network engineer, the role involves setting up, administering, maintaining and upgrading communication systems, local area networks and wide area networks for the organisation. I was also responsible for security, data storage and disaster recovery strategies. As a fresh graduate with a strong academic background in IT combined with excellent internship experience as a help-desk analyst. I have consistently learnt and built-up technical troubleshooting skills used to rapidly and cost-effectively resolve challenging technical issues. I have equally used my time while serving as an intern to quickly learn and master new technologies, tools and testing methodologies. Having spent a short time in the information technology world, I have come to a realisation that there is more to it than just completing a course in my field of study. I have taken out time to initiative and develop a deeper understanding of how I can use these skills in the tech work environments.
